How we help

Engagement

The greatest asset of any organisation or team is their people. In order to get the best out of each person, understanding their motivation, and supporting them to develop is critical. When the employee feels understood, involved, supported, given the opportunity to develop, and is working towards something they believe in, they are more engaged and provide more value.

Retention

The War for Talent is real. When you combine the number of growing, ambitious organisations with the shift in the modern, transient workforce, retaining talent you have invested in becomes a high priority. Building stronger relationships, providing more opportunities for growth in line with the individuals motivation and being more flexible is now vital to keep your best people.

Performance

Individual and team culture are critical to performance. An environment where the workforce are comfortable to share, to learn, to collaborate and to challenge will lead to creativity, innovation and ownership. Individuals behaving like leaders, and taking responsibility to deliver quickly and with high quality is now essential. A coaching culture that praises effort over performance will produce more sustainable results.

Our Services

MINDSET FOR LEADING

Through a blended learning approach, we provide leaders with planning, implementation and reflection tools to drive impact in employee engagement, productivity, team motivation, team effectiveness and practically develop growth-minded, principle-centred cultures.


LEADING SELF

We apply the insights of relevant diagnostic and psychometric tools, with an understanding of perception, projection and preferences to team and individual based practices that drive impact on personal effectiveness and growth, employee engagement and communication.


LEADING OTHERS

Typically through tailored leading and team-focused simulations, we create scenarios where the need for emotional intelligence is high when both working as and leading and managing teams that drive impact on personal and team effectiveness, employee engagement, relationships, communication and customer satisfaction.

LEADING
LEADERS

Spotlighting ambiguity, complexity, pressure and changing demands, we use coaching scenarios and leadership simulations to drive impact on personal and team effectiveness, productivity, employee engagement, innovation and change management.


LEADERSHIP & TALENT FRAMEWORKS

If your organisation has an existing framework for leadership or talent development, we can plug into it by adapting the content from Mindset through to Leading Leaders. Through our experience in designing adult learning programmes, we can also work with you to design the most effective leadership development framework to meet your objectives.

COACHING &
COACH DEVELOPMENT

To meet the needs of today’s workforce, managers and leaders are required to coach. We help organisations to develop and improve their coaching capacity in-house, through designing focussed coach development programmes. In addition, we offer individual coaching support for all our partners.
