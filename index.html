How we help

Engagement

Every organization is made up of layers of teams. We believe that for any organization to perform at its highest level, they must have a shared vision, agreed values, and effective communication. For the modern business, where organizational design is changing from function to a more fluid nature, effective, collaborative teams are critical for success.

Retention

Every team needs a leader. Except leadership is changing. It is no longer a hierarchical, top-down pursuit. We believe that everyone can influence and impact on other people in the organization and demonstrate leadership in their own style. If a team or an organization has leaders with a winning mindset, it stands a greater chance of success.

Performance

To create high performance, coaching is imperative. The ability to support and develop a connected and powerful vision; to develop culture; to construct winning teams; to create meaningful relationships; to understand and support everyone’s unique motivations and to give regular, constructive feedback – are key factors in being a successful coach.

Our Services

INSIGHTS DISCOVERY

We are accredited to deliver Insights Discovery, and use the tools to build self-awareness, emotional intelligence and leadership capacity in individuals, and develop meaningful relationships, understand strengths and improve management in performing teams.

CHARACTERSCOPE

Trigger Leadership have partnered with Characterscope, a tool which allows a unique insight into an individual’s leadership style. Our effective facilitation and coaching allows leaders to maximize their strengths and enhance their development areas.

HIGH PERFORMING TEAMS

Once we understand the organizational objectives, we work to identify a teams’ strengths and its blind spots. We facilitate experiences that allow a team to lead from within, create its own vision and values; accountability; clear roles and improved performance.

EMPLOYEE ENGAGEMENT & EXPERIENCE

Through developing an understanding of the motivations of the individual, and what role they must play in the organization or team, we develop mindset; self-understanding; empathy; communication; leadership within a personal development strategy.

LEADERSHIP PROGRAMMES & TALENT DEVELOPMENT

We plug into an organisations existing leadership framework, or help them design an effective strategy or framework to enhance their culture and achieve their objectives. We use e-learning; 360’ feedback; psychometrics and performance metrics to support our work.

COACHING & COACH DEVELOPMENT

We support individual leaders through mentoring and coaching. We establish goals for the relationship, through personal or organizational motivations. We help organisations develop their coaching capacity in-house, through coach development programmes.
